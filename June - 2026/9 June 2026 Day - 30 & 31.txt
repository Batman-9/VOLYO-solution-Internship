**Day 30 & Day 31: Database Selection, Design Review & Introduction to NoSQL**

### Topic 1: Database Selection & Design Review

**Learning Objectives:**

* Understand how to select the most suitable database for different business requirements
* Learn the basic concept of the CAP Theorem (Consistency, Availability, Partition Tolerance)
* Analyze factors such as scalability, performance, reliability, and data structure requirements
* Evaluate relational and non-relational databases based on real-world scenarios
* Develop decision-making skills for database architecture design

**Activities / Deliverables:**

* Analyze three Volyo business scenarios and identify the most suitable database solution for each
* Justify database selection based on business requirements, scalability, and performance needs
* Research the CAP Theorem and understand its practical implications
* Prepare a presentation summarizing findings and recommendations
* Document decision-making criteria used for database selection

**Tools / Tech:**

* Research Resources
* Presentation Software

### Topic 2: Introduction to NoSQL

**Learning Objectives:**

* Understand why NoSQL databases were introduced and their advantages
* Learn the four major NoSQL database types:

  * Document Databases
  * Key-Value Databases
  * Column-Family Databases
  * Graph Databases
* Explore real-world applications and use cases of each NoSQL model
* Compare NoSQL databases with traditional RDBMS systems
* Understand when NoSQL is preferred over relational databases

**Activities / Deliverables:**

* Research different NoSQL database categories and their characteristics
* Create a comparison chart between RDBMS and the four NoSQL database types
* Study real-world examples such as MongoDB, Redis, Cassandra, and Neo4j
* Analyze strengths, limitations, and ideal use cases for each database model
* Present findings and discuss database selection strategies for modern applications

**Tools / Tech:**

* Draw.io
* Research Resources
